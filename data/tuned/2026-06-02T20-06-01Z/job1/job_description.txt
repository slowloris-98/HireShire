About the Role

You will work on translating state-of-the-art research into scalable, real-world AI systems, including virtual humans, text-to-image, and text-to-video generation. You will collaborate closely with research, engineering, and product teams to advance next-generation multimodal and generative AI technologies.

Build AIGC systems, including virtual humans, text-to-image, and text-to-video models

Convert cutting-edge research into scalable, production-ready AI systems
