About the job

The client's platform connects domain experts with AI labs and enterprises to train models and build reliable AI agents.

scalability.

• Contribute to project planning and offer insights on technical feasibility and solutions.
• Communicate effectively with team members and stakeholders, both in writing and verbally.


Required Skills & Qualifications:

• Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for building responsive and user-friendly websites.
• Experience with integrating backend APIs and handling data exchange between frontend and backend services.

• Familiarity with industry best practices for writing clean, well-documented, and maintainable code.

This role offers a unique opportunity to work with a global leader in AI, contributing to the development of frontier AI models. You will be part of a global network of experts working together to create training data, evaluations, and feedback loops that improve how AI systems perform.
